About this page

This blog was origionally supposed to be about me learning how to manage an ESXI server. Since my background is primarily DF and cyber, I am not so good at the networking and sysadmin side of things. That being said, at work, some things came up where I needed to go and learn how to do all most of the management side of things, including vswitches, portgroups, and most other features of ESXI. So instead, I have decided to make this blog about how I am building my homelab, and kind of passing along some of the knowledge I have learned onto others. If you would like to copy what I am doing, feel free! Disclaimer though, I do not know everything by any means about this subject, and a lot of this is new to me. If you think I could be doing anything better, feel free to reach out to me on twitter or discord!
