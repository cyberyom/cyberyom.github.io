About Me

CyberYom Autobiography

Hello! My name is Tom Claflin, and I am a 21 year old from Burlington, Vermont. I am a Champlain College Alumni, I currently work with the Leahy Center, a division of Champlain College, doing IoT forensics and Project Management. On top of this, I work as a DFIR Security Researcher for a small Digital Forensics training company called Cyber5W.

My research interests include DFIR, Malware Analysis, IoT offensive security, ransomware trends, and threat intel/hunting. This blog will be concerned with most of these, as well as some new concepts that I am planning on looking into, such as C coding, reverse engineering, and ESXI management.

Feel free to reach out to me via twitter, linkedin, or discord to start a dialogue or give me tips! As a younger person in the DFIR world, I am ALWAYS looking for advice and constructive criticism, so that is always welcome!
